Pontoon Hills Tour

Pontoon is lovely spot in East Mayo with undulating hills and beutiful lakes. The hike will bring you up into the hills overlooking the stunning surrounding area. After walking through the forest and hiking up through the hills you will see Lough Cong and Mount Nephin. It's a great viewpoint and a nice spot for our coffee and snack break.


Price

  • Children under 18: €20
  • Adults: €25

What to expect!

  • Duration: 3.5hrs
  • moderate
  • tea/coffee/snack break

What to bring!

  • Good hiking runners/boots
  • All weather gear
  • A hunger for a bit of adventure
