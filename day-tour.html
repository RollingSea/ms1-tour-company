Pontoon Hills Tour

Pontoon is lovely spot in East Mayo with undulating hills and beutiful lakes. The hike will bring you up into the hills overlooking the stunning surrounding area. After walking through the forest and hiking up through the hills you will see Lough Cong and Mount Nephin. It's a great viewpoint to take in the surrounding area and a nice spot for our coffee and snack break.

  • Adults: €25

  • moderate
  • tea/coffee/snack break

Lake Isle of Inishfree

Follow in the footsteps of W.B. Yeats and explore the area aound Slish Wood on the Sligo Way with us. This tour brings you along the beautiful shores of Lough Gill, through the ancient Slish Woods and up into the hills where you will get a panoramic view of Lough Gill and it's many island. This is where W.B. Yeats came to relax and write and you might do the same.

Lough Talt, Ox Mountains

This is a beautiful gem hidden away in the hills of the Ox Mountains, known locally as the Windy Gap. Lough Talt is surrounded by beautiful alpine flora and fauna, grazing mountain sheep, old stone cottages and even it's own wishing well. This tour will take us along the lakes edge and up into the ancient hills with a beautiful view overlooking the lake and surrounding area.

Streedagh Beach

Walk along Streedagh Beach with a view of Ben Bulben, ancient fossils and the historic ship wrecks of the Spanish Armada. This is a place steeped in history and absolutley beautiful to look at too. On your guided walk you will find out all bout it's history, while listening too the crashing waves and hopefully soaking up some sunshine. You are welcome to bring your swim gear and take a dip during our break.

Strandhill Beach

Strandhill known for it's fantastic surf is a stunning spot for a Beach Tour. With views into Ballisodare Bay, a seal collony and amazing waves this place has a lot to offer. Our tour will bring us from the village towards the seal collony. With a little luck you will spot a few of them. The surrounding area is beautiful and a great spot to watch the sun play as it highlights the hills in the distance.

Mulranny

Mulranny is a small town on the edge of Clew Bay not far from Achill and Newport. We will be exploring the coast at low tide where we will be able to wander between the many small islands that Clew Bay is famous for. With views of Croagh Patrick and Westport this is a magical place. There is also a great place to eat called Nevin's Bar, perfect if your feeling hungry after your day out.
