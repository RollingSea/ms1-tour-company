About Us

Founded in 2016 by Frieda Harrington, Rolling Hills Adventure Company has established itself as the leading tour company along the Wild Atlantic Way in the West of Ireland. Frieda completed her Masters in Outdoor Education in 2012, after which she travelled accross asia for a year. She then worked with an tour company in New Zealand for two years. On her return to her homeplace in South Sligo she took the opportunity to set up her own tour company. Frieda knows the area like the back of her hand. The terrain, the beauty spots and the history. Booking a tour with Frieda is an immersive experience filled with adventure and fun.
